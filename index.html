Anambra State

Anambra, whose motto is the "Light of the Nation," is a state in southeastern Nigeria known for its vibrant culture, industrious populace (predominantly Igbo), and strong commercial significance. Its capital is Awka, while Onitsha serves as a major commercial hub with one of the largest markets in West Africa, and Nnewi is a rapidly developing industrial city. The state has a rich history dating back to the 9th century AD, notably the ancient Kingdom of Nri, and boasts a high literacy rate and significant contributions to Nigerian academia, business, and politics. With natural resources including crude oil and natural gas, and an economy driven by agriculture, trade, and industry, Anambra is one of Nigeria's most urbanized and densely populated states. ©2026 ❖ Madeleine Nkiru Anagha ❖ Anambra, Nigeria.
